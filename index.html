Audio Image Map Demo

This is a demo of a website allowing users to play audio by clicking on areas in an image. Click some of the objects being held in the image above. This has been tested on Safari and Chrome browsers.

By clicking the Christmas tree, snowman, star or Santa, you will start one of four audio clips. Clicking the object area again will stop the audio. Go to the tutorial Toggle Play and Pause on Multiple Audio Files by Clicking Areas on an Image Map
